We participated in a voluntary work program with other 12 volunteers from all over the world. The program targeted kindergarten to school children from very poor background with very high criminality. Besides helping witth everyday activities in the kindergarten, participating in play time and learning activities, I also sew a basic pattern of a bathing robe for children participting in surfing camp. The pattern was then used for producing 20 new bathing robes. We hope we made a little difference in their lives....
